Help

Aim of the game

Be the first player to connect 4 of their counters in a row horizontally, vertically or diagonally.

How to play

  1. Select if the game is going to be against the computer or another player
  2. Each player will take turns placing a counter of their colour of red or yellow
  3. When it is your turn click on the spot you wish to place, please note that if the move is invalid you will be alerted and you will then be asked to place again in a valid place
  4. The disc will then occupy the cell and turn will change to the next player
  5. Players will take turns until one wins with 4 of their coloured discs in a row horizontally, verticall or horizontally. If all positions have been taken and there is no winner the game will be declared a draw.
  6. Chose to restart game or quit.

Controls

Controls are based on mouse clicks on finger presses if on a touchscreen device.

Session Stats

Each game sessions statistics will be counted and can be located below the game board. These statistics will reset upon page reload or closure.
