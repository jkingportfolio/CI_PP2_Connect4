Help

Aim of the game

Be the first player to connect 4 of their counters in a row horizontally, vertically or diagonally.

How to play

  1. Select if the game is going to be against the computer or another player from the New Game modal
  2. Each player will take turns placing a counter, whos turn it is will be indicated above the game board
  3. When it is your turn click on the cell you wish to place your counter, please note that if the move is invalid you will be alerted and should pick a new cell to place your counter.
  4. The counter will then occupy the cell and player turn will change to the next player.
  5. Players will take turns until one wins with 4 of their coloured counters in a row horizontally, verticall or horizontally. If all positions have been taken and there is no winner the game will be declared a draw.
  6. Players can then chose to start a new game from the Play again button.

Controls

Controls are based on mouse clicks or finger presses if using a touchscreen device.

Session Stats

Each game sessions statistics will be counted and can be located below the game board. These statistics will reset upon page reload or closure.
